Mandavli Deluxe Sanctuary

A profound embrace of heritage and tranquility, designed as a restorative retreat from the modern world.

Sanctuary of Repose

The Mandavli Deluxe Sanctuary is more than a suite; it is a curated experience of stillness. Inspired by the traditional 'Mandav' architecture of Gujarat, the room features expansive volumes and natural materials that breathe with the rhythm of the resort.

Every corner is designed to facilitate 'Shanti'—the internal peace that comes from being perfectly at home. From the hand-woven textiles to the artisanal woodwork, the Sanctuary reflects a profound respect for the slow, intentional life.

"Where heritage meets the soul's silence."
